Departments

New Haven Roleplay offers structured, realistic departments designed for serious roleplay. Each department follows real-world procedures, training standards, and chain of command.

Law Enforcement

LSPD

Los Santos Police Department — urban policing, traffic enforcement, investigations, and city patrol operations.

BCSO

Blaine County Sheriff’s Office — rural patrol, highway response, warrant service, and county-wide law enforcement.

SAST

San Andreas State Troopers — highway patrol, traffic enforcement, vehicle interdiction, and statewide operations.

Fire & Medical

SAFR

San Andreas Fire Rescue — fire suppression, EMS response, rescue operations, and medical roleplay.

Subdivisions

SWAT

Special Weapons and Tactics unit handling high-risk situations. SWAT is available exclusively to BCSO members.

Additional subdivisions will be introduced as departments expand.
